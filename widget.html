GBP Siding Growth Playbook — 30-Day Sprint

(for multi-trade contractors)

Goal: Shift topical relevance and conversions toward Siding in 30 days—without creating a duplicate GBP.

Executive Summary

  • Keep one GBP. Drive “siding” clarity using categories, services, products, photos, posts, reviews, Q&A, and website alignment.
  • Temporarily set Primary Category = Siding contractor and deep-link GBP to your /siding page for 60–90 days.
  • Measure with UTM tags, GBP Insights, and lead source notes.

1) Categories (highest leverage)

  • Primary: Siding contractor (flip during siding push; you can switch back seasonally).
  • Secondary: keep Roofing contractor, add General contractor (and legit subs like Window installation service if applicable).
  • Mirror competitors’ categories when legitimate.

2) Services (structured & keyword-aligned)

  • Vinyl siding installation / replacement
  • Fiber-cement siding (James Hardie)
  • Siding repair (storm, hail, single-elevation)
  • Soffit & fascia / aluminum wrap
  • Cedar shake siding
  • Insulated siding; house wrap & flashing

3) Products (visual tiles that convert)

  • Full Siding Replacement — Free Estimate
  • James Hardie ColorPlus® Install
  • Vinyl Siding + Soffit Package

4) Links (point GBP at siding)

  • Website link → /siding (not homepage).
  • Appointment link → short “Siding Estimate” form.
  • Use UTM: ?utm_source=gbp&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=siding

5) Photos & Video (fresh, labeled)

  • Upload 3–5 siding photos weekly (before/after; trim/soffit close-ups).
  • Captions like “Vinyl siding replacement — Paramus NJ”.
  • Add a 20–45s vertical site walk-through video.

6) Posts (weekly)

Example: “Hardie Spring Special — Free Color Consult” → link to /siding. Use a photo, one benefit bullet, and a simple CTA.

7) Reviews that mention “siding”

After each siding job, prompt: “What work did we do?” Reply once using the word siding naturally.

8) Q&A (seed genuine questions)

  • “Do you install James Hardie fiber-cement siding?”
  • “Can you match existing vinyl?”
  • “How long does full siding replacement take?”

9) Service Area & Citations

  • Keep service areas realistic.
  • Update major directories so descriptions mention siding alongside roofing. Keep NAP consistent.

10) Website Alignment

  • Strong /siding page: real project photos, towns, brand badges, FAQs, testimonials, short estimate form.
  • Internal links from homepage and roofing pages → /siding using natural anchors.
  • Add `LocalBusiness` schema and include “siding” in `serviceType`.

30-Day Execution Plan

  • Week 1: Flip primary → Siding contractor; update categories/services/products; set links to /siding; 2 posts; 8–10 photos; add 3 Q&As.
  • Week 2: 1 short siding video; 1 post; request 3 siding-specific reviews.
  • Week 3: Add 6–8 photos; expand /siding FAQs; add town list; internal links.
  • Week 4: 1 post; reply to reviews with “siding” once; evaluate Insights and adjust.

Measurement

  • GBP Insights: growth in siding queries; Actions (Calls, Website visits).
  • Analytics: UTM sessions and form fills on /siding.
  • CRM: tag leads “Siding” to quantify result.

Risks & Mitigations

  • Roofing lead dip while Primary = Siding. Mitigate with LSAs/Search ads for roofing; switch back after the sprint.
  • Thin siding page. Add gallery, FAQs, brands, and neighborhoods quickly.
  • Few siding reviews. Proactively request; capture specifics in the ask.
